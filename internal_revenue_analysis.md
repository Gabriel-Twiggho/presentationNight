## AI-Attributable vs Full Revenue

For companies with non-AI business lines, we separate:

- **Nvidia**: ~91% AI (Data Center segment)
- **Microsoft**: ~25% AI (Azure AI portion of Intelligent Cloud)
- **Oracle**: ~29% AI (Cloud Infrastructure AI portion)
- **AMD**: ~41% AI (Data Center AI accelerators)
- **Intel**: ~9% AI (Gaudi and AI-specific DCAI)

Pure-play AI companies (OpenAI, CoreWeave, Nebius, xAI, Figure AI, Mistral, Nscale, Anysphere, Ambience, Harvey) = 100% AI revenue.
