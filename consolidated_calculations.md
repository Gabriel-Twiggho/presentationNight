## Key Observations

1. **Revenue Growth**: Consolidated outside revenue (full) grew from $355B in 2023 to $489B in 2025 LTM (+37.7% cumulative)

2. **AI Revenue Growth**: AI-attributable revenue grew from $84B to $170B (+102.4% cumulative), significantly outpacing full revenue

3. **EV Expansion**: Enterprise value nearly doubled from $4.17T to $8.28T (+98.6% cumulative)

4. **Valuation Multiple Expansion**: EV/Revenue (full) expanded from 11.7x to 16.9x, indicating valuation growing faster than revenue

5. **AI Multiple**: EV/Revenue on AI-only basis remains elevated at ~49x, relatively stable over the period
